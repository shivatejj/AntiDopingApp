Gamified Anti-Doping Quiz

What is Anti-Doping?

Definition: Anti-doping refers to the policies and regulations put in place to prevent the use of performance-enhancing drugs in sports.

Objective: To ensure fair play and the health of athletes.

The Risks of Doping

Physical Risks: Serious health issues, including heart problems and hormonal imbalances.

Mental Risks: Anxiety, depression, and a diminished sense of well-being.

Commonly Banned Substances

Anabolic Steroids: Used to increase muscle mass and performance.

Stimulants: Enhance alertness and endurance.
